Beaver Mines


Nestled in the Crowsnest Pass is Beaver Mines, located in the foothills of southern Alberta. It offers great views of the surrounding mountains and easy access to the large lake located within the area. The forest offers great protection for campers and creates a natural habit for many of the local wildlife who live there. You can see many of them during a visit to these scenic campsites.
